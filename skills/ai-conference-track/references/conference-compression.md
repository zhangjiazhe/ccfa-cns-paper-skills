# Conference Compression

Use this when an AI conference paper exceeds page limits or reads like a full journal manuscript.

## Compression Order

1. Remove tutorial background the venue audience already knows.
2. Merge paragraphs that make the same point.
3. Replace generic adjectives with numbers or concrete claims.
4. Move dense secondary results to appendix.
5. Convert process descriptions into claim-first topic sentences.
6. Keep only the best example for each argument.
7. Promote lists of numbers into figures or tables when that improves scanability.

## Do Not Compress Away

- the problem gap
- method novelty
- enough experimental setup to judge fairness
- key ablations
- limitations and reproducibility-critical details

## Report

When compressing, report:
- original length and new length
- cuts made by category
- risks introduced by compression
- appendix moves
